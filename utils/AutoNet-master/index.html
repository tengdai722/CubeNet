AutoNet System

The AutoNet system can turn domain-specific corpora in structured networks without additional expert effort. This interface is for the online discovery and exploration.

1. Construction on the Fly: Put the URL to your corpus and click the upload button. The constructed network will be visualized on the left panel.

2. Keyword Exploration: Type your keywords and click the search button. The retrieved network will be visualized on the left panel.

General Tips

  • Click the expand button will retrieve 32 more related nodes if there are
  • Hover over a node or an edge to see the entity names, and to highlight related entities.
  • Click on a node or an edge to show entity details. It will open up a side panel.
  • On the details panel, you can click on the target icon to center the graph on that movie. And clicking on the link to a related entity will move to that entity (in the graph and in the panel)
  • You can use you usual browser controls to zoom and pan over the graph. If you zoom in when centred on a entity, the graph will expand around it.
  • Above a certain zoom level, entity names are automatically displayed for all the nodes.
